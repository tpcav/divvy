Automatically split monthly payments between two or more people.

Frequently Asked Questions

Shared payments are made possible by using mutiple people's form of payments to add money to an online debit card based off pertanges defined before purchase.

Let's say you want to split a monthly payment with three friends that cost $30/month. In that case, then each of your forms of payment would be charged $10 a month to equal the total $30 needed.

You can cancel out of a shared payment plan through your subscription settings. As long as the other parties do not object.

This service can be used for other kinds of payments. No need to wait for those people to pay you back that owe you money.
